Google Merchandise Traffic Analysis

Data Analytics Project - 2023

Data Analytics focuses on gaining insights about current situations by analyzing datasets from past to present, enabling informed decision-making and solution development for existing problems.

This case study analyzes traffic from the Google Merchandise Demo Account.

Project Overview

Goal

Companies often possess data containing hidden information. Asking the right questions and analyzing data reveals relevant insights. The goal is to answer questions that help companies make informed decisions.

Result

Results are presented in a report with recommendations and follow-up steps. Based on this report, clear, well-founded decisions can be made, costs can be saved, and processes optimized.

Duration

Project duration ranges from 2 weeks to 2 months. Completion time is fastest when data is readily available and properly cleaned.

Case: Optimizing the Sales Funnel

The website owner wanted to identify where users drop out during the ordering process and whether distinctions could be made between different customer types. According to the analysis, optimizing the sales funnel could potentially increase revenue by 5.8%.

Number of Visitors

Comparing 2021-2022 data shows the website performed well - both new and returning users increased significantly between the years.

However, deeper examination revealed the e-commerce conversion rate actually dropped despite increased user numbers - an undesired outcome indicating fewer orders.

Sales Funnel Analysis

Google Analytics easily visualized the sales funnel, showing where visitors dropped out and how they behaved on the website.

The visualization shows significant visitor drop-off during the first funnel stage due to required sign-in. Improvements could include:

  • Implementing guest checkout feature
  • Fixing redirect behavior - when users sign in from cart, redirect back to cart instead of the store
  • These changes improve efficiency and website performance

Channel Analysis

Which channels are most used by users?

Channel analysis revealed major issues. While direct channels dominated, and paid search and display performed well, other channels underperformed. Improvements could include:

  • Enhancing social media presence through influencer partnerships
  • Optimizing underutilized channels with growth potential
  • Strategic influencer selection could dramatically increase sales
