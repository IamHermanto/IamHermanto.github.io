Sales Performance Dashboard

Business Intelligence Project - 2023

This dashboard example focuses on sales, revenue, and customer metrics. Three different views provide multiple perspectives on the data.

The executive summary shows overall important visualizations, product details showcase sold products, and customer details highlight top customers and their product contributions.

Project Overview

Goal

Dashboards enable viewing the most important KPIs and results at a glance. They provide an interactive way to see how companies or projects perform.

Result

Using dashboards, you discover insights easily. You can apply filters and consult different graphs. With immediate access to the right information, correct decisions can be made.

Duration

Dashboard development typically takes 2 weeks to 2 months, consisting of two phases:

  • KPI selection and dashboard design
  • Data collection and dashboard development
